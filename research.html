Customer Segments

HOT

Ready to Engage

Score 80-100. High awareness of coordination gaps, likely experiencing a triggering event. Ready for professional guidance.

Trigger events: Divorce or inheritance, business sale or exit, retirement within 2 years, IRS audit or tax shock, loss of a spouse, major health event in the family.

WARM

Considering Options

Score 50-79. Recognizes gaps, considering options, needs education and trust-building.

Signals: Knows they need help but not sure who to trust. Currently using a non-fiduciary advisor or managing alone. Searching for "fiduciary advisor near me" or "wealth management for families."

COLD

Problem-Aware

Score 0-49. Early awareness, may not realize the scope of coordination gaps.

Signals: Consuming financial content but not taking action. DIY investors who have outgrown their approach. "I should probably talk to someone about this."

Five Wealth Profiles

1. The Legacy Builder

Focused on multi-generational wealth transfer. Thinks in generational timelines. Needs coordinated estate planning.

2. The Work-Optional Seeker

Has built wealth but lacks a plan for transitioning to freedom. Needs cash flow planning and tax strategy.

3. The Wealth Protector

Values transparency and fiduciary duty. May have been burned before. Needs trust-based, conflict-free guidance.

4. The Strategic Grower

Growth-minded, often an entrepreneur or business owner. Needs to separate business wealth from personal wealth.

5. The Navigating Family

Going through a major life transition. Needs one team to coordinate everything during a period of change.

Key Competitive Threats

  • 2. Glenmede owns #1 for "wealth management philadelphia" with a similar heritage-based, family office narrative
  • 3. National RIA aggregators (Creative Planning, Cerity Partners) are aggressively acquiring Philadelphia-area firms
  • 4. Blue Bell PWM operates in the same Montgomery County geography

Lansing Street's Competitive Advantages

  • 1. Fiduciary Independence - Not beholden to any bank, wirehouse, or product company
  • 2. Media & Thought Leadership - Topley's Top 10 newsletter, media appearances, AdvisorHub recognition
  • 3. Real Estate Expertise - $50M Lansing Street Real Estate Fund (60+ transactions)
  • 4. Family Office at Boutique Scale - More personalized than myCIO ($14.4B) or Glenmede ($40B+)
