A complete lead generation system that attracts high-net-worth families, qualifies them by readiness, and nurtures them toward a discovery meeting with Lansing Street Advisors.

How It Works

The funnel guides prospects through a five-step journey from awareness to action.

1

Landing Page

The prospect arrives from an ad, social post, newsletter, or direct link. They see a compelling headline about their Wealth Strategy Score and click "Discover Your Score in 2 Minutes." No sign-up required to start.

2

7-Question Assessment

Seven questions across seven different interaction types (card selection, slider, tag cloud, emoji scale, multiple choice, yes/no toggle, star rating). Each question is specific to wealth coordination and maps to one of five radar dimensions. Takes approximately 2 minutes.

3

Email Capture

After all seven questions, the prospect enters their email to receive results. The email capture screen references the value waiting for them: their personalized score, radar chart, and actionable recommendations. Email is required; first name is optional.

4

Personalized Results

The results page displays: their score (0-100), a 5-dimension radar chart, their matched profile (one of five), a key insight, a hidden insight, three actionable tips, and temperature-appropriate CTAs. No pricing. Value first.

5

Email Nurture

Based on their score (hot 80-100, warm 50-79, cold 0-49), the prospect enters one of three tailored email sequences. Hot leads receive 2 consultation-focused emails. Warm leads get 3 educational + case study emails. Cold leads receive 4 trust-building educational emails. All paths include a welcome sequence and a win-back sequence for non-engagers.

3

Why It Works

This quiz funnel is built on proven psychological principles tailored to the wealth management industry.

Self-Diagnosis Creates Ownership

When prospects select answers that describe their coordination gaps, they are admitting the problem themselves. No sales copy is more powerful than a self-identified need.

Education Builds Trust

The quiz teaches prospects about financial coordination, fiduciary duty, and the four pillars. They leave the quiz smarter than they arrived, whether or not they become clients.

Personalization Feels Exclusive

The radar chart, profile match, and hidden insight make each result feel individually crafted. Prospects feel seen and understood by a firm that "gets" their specific situation.

Temperature Routing Matches Intent

Hot leads get direct consultation CTAs. Cold leads get educational content. Nobody feels pushed too hard or left behind. Each prospect gets the right next step for their readiness level.

Thought Leadership Integration

The funnel connects to Matt Topley's existing content ecosystem (Topley's Top 10 newsletter, View from the Top blog). Cold leads enter the content flywheel and warm up over time.

Heritage Creates Differentiation

The Kilkenny Castle narrative, family heritage story, and four pillars framework are woven into email sequences and results copy. No competitor can replicate this authentic brand identity.

Embed the quiz on the Lansing Street Advisors website. Exit-intent popup on key pages (financial services, about us). Sticky banner on the blog. Convert existing website traffic into qualified leads.
