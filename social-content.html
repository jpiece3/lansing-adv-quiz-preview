30-Day Social Content Calendar

Content strategy for promoting the Wealth Strategy Score quiz funnel across all platforms

Content Pillars

All social content rotates through four pillars derived from Lansing Street Advisors' core messaging and quiz keyword clusters.

1. The Coordination Gap

Educational content about fragmented financial lives, the cost of uncoordinated planning, and why a family office approach matters. Drives quiz starts.

2. Fiduciary Trust

Content about fiduciary duty, the difference between brokers and advisors, and why independence matters. Builds brand differentiation.

3. Generational Wealth

Statistics and stories about multi-generational wealth preservation, estate planning, and family governance. Emotionally resonant for Legacy Builders.

4. Work-Optional Life

Content about financial freedom, retirement readiness, and making work a choice. Connects directly to the Lansing Street mission and quiz outcomes.

Platform Strategy

LINKEDIN

Primary Channel

Matt Topley's personal profile. Long-form thought leadership posts (150-300 words). Market commentary tied to coordination themes. 3-4 posts per week. Matt's personal voice. Quiz link in every other post.

INSTAGRAM

Visual Education

Carousel posts with statistics and frameworks. Story polls about wealth coordination. Reels with quick tips. Brand colors (navy/gold). 3-4 posts per week. Link in bio to quiz.

TWITTER/X

Market Commentary

Quick market observations, financial stats, and one-liners from Topley's Top 10. Thread format for deeper topics. 4-5 posts per week. Quiz link pinned.

FACEBOOK

Community / Local

Share Topley's Top 10 content, quiz link, local community engagement. Target Montgomery County and Philadelphia area audiences. 2-3 posts per week.

Content Guidelines

Voice: Authoritative yet approachable. Write as Matt Topley on LinkedIn and Twitter. Write as the Lansing Street brand on Instagram and Facebook. Never use aggressive sales language. Always lead with value.
Quiz link frequency: Include a quiz link in approximately 60% of posts. The other 40% should be pure value (no CTA). This ratio builds trust and prevents fatigue.
Visual consistency: Instagram carousels and stories should use the navy (#1B365D) and gold (#B29D62) brand palette. Playfair Display for headlines. Source Sans for body text. Clean, premium layouts that reflect the Glossy design mode.
