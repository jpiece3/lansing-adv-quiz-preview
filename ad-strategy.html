A/B TESTING

A/B Testing Recommendations

TestVariable AVariable BSuccess Metric
Headline angle "What's Your Wealth Strategy Score?" "How Coordinated Is Your Financial Life?" Click-through rate
Social proof vs. curiosity "$500M AUM, SEC-registered fiduciary" (authority) "7 questions, 2 minutes, personalized radar chart" (curiosity) Click-through rate
Pain vs. aspiration "Coordination gaps may be costing you $30K+/year" (pain) "Do you know your work-optional number?" (aspiration) Quiz completion rate
CTA button text "Take the Assessment" "Get Your Free Score" Click-through rate
Landing page length Full landing page (benefits, social proof, how it works) Short page (headline, subheadline, CTA only) Quiz start rate
Testing priority: Start with headline angle testing (highest impact on CTR). Run each test for a minimum of 500 impressions per variant before drawing conclusions. Rotate winning headlines into other platforms.
